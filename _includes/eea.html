This lesson is being developed under the initiative and sponsorship of the Standing Committee on Research of the European Economic Association (EEA). The EEA is an international scientific body, with membership open to all persons involved or interested in economics. Individual membership of the EEA includes access to the Journal of the Economic Association (JEEA). Members can submit papers to JEEA for no fee. Members also have the right to submit a paper to the EEA annual congress and attend the event.
